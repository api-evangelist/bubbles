TL;DR: A lean business plan is a 1-3 page document covering your business concept, target market, revenue model, and key goals. It helps you validate ideas quickly and make early decisions without extensive research. Best for early-stage startups, team alignment, and initial investor conversations, it’s a living document that evolves as you learn. Starting a business begins with an idea: a product you’ve imagined, a problem you want to solve, or people you want to help.
