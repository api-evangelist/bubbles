Document every step someone takes manually: where they get information, what decisions they make, which systems they touch, and who they hand off to next.</p><p>Look for bottlenecks where work sits waiting. Maybe provisioning requests wait in email for approvals, or incident tickets go unnoticed for hours after alerts fire. These delays show you where automation delivers the biggest impact.</p><p>Define what success looks like with specific metrics. If you&#x2019;re automating user provisioning, measure how long it takes from request to completion before and after automation.
